Ledgerline Onboarding Revamp

RoleLead Product Designer
Team SizePM + 6 engineers
Timeframe10 weeks
PlatformWeb app
StatusShipped

Problem

Compliance-heavy onboarding caused a 2-step abandonment spike after KYC document upload.

Ownership

Owned IA, UX writing, onboarding flows, and handoff specs across design and compliance.

Approach

  • Audited analytics and support tickets to isolate friction points.
  • Mapped compliance requirements into a simplified task flow.
  • Prototyped 3 variants with focus on progress clarity.
  • Aligned copy with legal for transparent outcomes.
  • Partnered with engineering on post-KYC success states.

Key Decisions

  • Added pre-KYC checklist to reduce surprise requirements.
  • Bundled optional steps into a post-approval flow.

Outcomes

Improved activation by ~X% (baseline unknown—replace). Reduced support tickets by ~X%.

Takeaway: Transparency and staging compliance work increased trust and throughput.

Team SizePM + 4 engineers
Timeframe8 weeks
PlatformDesktop web
StatusShipped

Problem

Ownership

Approach

  • Standardize case notes for audit readiness.
